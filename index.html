On-device face AI

FaceNet-512 runs entirely on your phone via TFLite. Your face data never leaves the device.

Instant recognition

MediaPipe detection + vector search in SQLite. Matches in under a second, even with hundreds of entries.

🏷️

Vibe tags

Ghost Type. Red Flag. Wild Card. Tag people with archetypes that actually make sense.

🔒

Zero cloud

No account. No sync. No servers. Everything lives in a local SQLite database on your device.

📸

Multi-photo matching

Add multiple reference photos per person. The app uses the closest match, handling angle and lighting variation.

🔍

Search by face or name

Point your camera, pick from gallery, or just type a name. All on the same screen.
